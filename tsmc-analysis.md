# TSMC Analysis

## Company Overview
TSMC is the world’s leading semiconductor foundry.

## Key Strengths
- Advanced chip manufacturing (3nm, 2nm)
- Strong clients: Apple, NVIDIA
- High margins

## Risks
- China–Taiwan geopolitical risk
- High capital expenditure

## My View
TSMC is a long-term compounder but needs monitoring due to geopolitical risks.
