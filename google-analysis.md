# Google (Alphabet) Analysis

## Company Overview
Alphabet (Google) is one of the largest technology companies in the world, with businesses across search, advertising, cloud computing, and AI.

## Core Businesses
- Google Search (main revenue driver)
- YouTube
- Google Cloud
- Android ecosystem

## Strengths
- Dominant search engine globally
- Strong advertising business with high margins
- Growing cloud segment
- Massive data advantage for AI

## Revenue Model
- Majority from digital advertising
- Increasing contribution from cloud services

## Risks
- Competition in AI (OpenAI, Microsoft)
- Regulatory risks (antitrust)
- Dependence on advertising revenue
- Shift in user behavior (AI replacing search)

## Financial Quality
- Strong cash flows
- High margins
- Large cash reserves

## My View
Google is a strong long-term business with a dominant position in search, but faces challenges from AI disruption and regulatory pressure.

## Conclusion
A high-quality company with strong fundamentals, but needs to adapt successfully to the AI shift to maintain its dominance.
